What happens in the movie

  • Road Runner floats in the air
  • Eyeballs fall out
  • Small stick supports a boulder and then it falls directly on Wile E. Coyote
  • Tablecloth goes over hole without making an indent for the empty space
  • TNT explodes without affecting surroundings
  • Coyote cuts down entire bridge and mountain by cutting out a circle of the bridge
  • Metal stretches

What should have happened

  • Road Runner was not using his wings so shouldn't be able to float like that
  • Wile E. Coyote's eyes cannot just fall out. Even if they did he couldn't just put them back in and be able to use them again
  • The tablecloth should sink in where the hole is
  • The explosion should have debris around it and stuff should be falling back down
  • It just does not work like that. The hole that was cut should have fallen down
  • The metal would not even bend from this scenario. Even if it did, the metal would only take the form of Wile E. Coyote's features that first hit the wall
