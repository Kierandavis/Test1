What happens in the movie

  • Robin Hood Daffy falls straight
  • Robin Hood Daffy spins with the same velocity his staff had when the man puts a stick in his way
  • Robin Hood Daffy is thrown forrward by his bow and arrow and then carries a tree effortlessly

What should have happened

  • Robin Hood Daffy has a horizontal velocity so therefore he would not be able to just fall straight down
  • The staff would just stop moving
  • He would not be shot forward by the bow. Average tree weighs 1,000,000 grams. Daffy would have to hold up the tree with a force of 10,000,000 N. Human can't even do this let alone a duck.
